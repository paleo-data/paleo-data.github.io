This page lists guidelines, or more formalized documents, that we have published for distribution and reuse by the broader community.

<i class='fa fa-star fa-lg'></i> ___[Guidelines for Using Wikidata to Mobilize Information about People in Collections: A Paleontology Perspective](https://paleo-data.github.io/guidelines-wikidata-people/)___. The purpose of this document is to provide a framework for how to mobilize information via Wikidata about people working in and/or associated with scientific collections. Building on [previous Wikidata documentation produced by Siobhan Leachman in 2020](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4724139), participants of the Using Wikidata to Capture and Share Information about People in Paleontology workshop (held March 29-31, 2022) created this framework to formalize and share practical knowledge gained from the workshop.
